Networking Basics

What is Network ?

A network consists of two or more computers that are linked in order to share resources (such as Internet, Data, printers and CD-ROMs), exchange files, or allow electronic communications. The computers on a network may be linked through cables, telephone lines, radio waves, satellites.

Types of Network

The Three basic types of networks are:

1. Local Area Network (LAN)
2. Metropolitan Area Network (MAN)
3. Wide Area Network (WAN)
